If two people started loving each other deeply in a crazy obscessive way their relashionship will be over immediately.

Now there’s nothing wrong with this relashionship, it’s essencial for couples to enjoy the company of each other and love and trust each other deeply, because this is how god created us. we we want to be loved as mush as we want love, the brain releases oxytocin everytime we hug someone we love even our pets experience the same chemical responce which is **Activation of Reward Pathways**: Falling in love triggers the release of neurotransmitters such as dopamine, serotonin, and oxytocin in the brain's reward pathways. These neurotransmitters are associated with pleasure, reward, and bonding, leading to feelings of euphoria and happiness. So basically, you become addicted to the person you love because everytime you get a validation from your partner you get these happy hormone doses that keeps you attached to that person.
